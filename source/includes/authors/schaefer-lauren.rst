   Lauren Hayward Schaefer is a developer advocate for MongoDB. She began her career as a software 
   engineer for IBM where she held a variety of roles including full-stack developer, test 
   automation specialist, social media lead, and growth hacking engineer. She went on to be a 
   developer advocate at SugarCRM. She is an international speaker who is skilled in taking 
   hard-to-understand topics and making them seem simple. Lauren holds a BS and MS in Computer 
   Science from North Carolina State University and is the co-inventor of twelve issued United States patents.
