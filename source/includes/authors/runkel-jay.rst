   Jay Runkel is a Master Solutions Architect at MongoDB and has been working with 
   Fortune 500 companies to architect enterprise solutions using NoSQL document 
   databases for over 10 years. Before MongoDB, Runkel was a Principal Technologist 
   at MarkLogic where he worked with Financial Services, Medical, and Media 
   organizations to develop operational systems for analytics and custom publishing. 
   Runkel has also held various positions developing encryption asset management, 
   automated underwriting, product information management, and CRM solutions. 
   Runkel has a BS in Applied Mathematics from Carnegie Mellon and a Masters in 
   Computer Science from the University of Michigan.
